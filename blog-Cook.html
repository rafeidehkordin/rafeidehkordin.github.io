This Is The Delicious Part

February 03, 2020

When I moved to USA I didn’t have any idea of cooking. At that time cooking was the most annoying thing to do for me, I used to see it as a task that needs to be done to survive and it took a lot of time. I had a friend he was really good at cooking, Mehdi. One day I was so desperate talking to him how I am terrible at cooking, he told me his story of how he became really good at cooking, and how hard was it for him as well, but now it’s a hobby. So, I started do cooking as a hobby, as something to get a way of life routine it helps manage your anxiety, gradually it became more and more fun. Now I cook, to have a healthier life, and I do research for every single material in it. Their nutrition, their effect on health and other benefits of them on mind, body and finally I read to learn how to make them more delicious, since it needs attention and love to make it delicious it requires a degree of focus which is soothing for me.

But…….. Baking is what I used to be really good at since I was a teenager. For me, baking, is a form of self-expression. Baking to me is, dancing or listening to a peaceful piano melody. I know what my friends love and they know whenever I am in a party there are cakes and cookies. And here I am to share some of my cakes, bread, dessert, and foods that are Healthy. That’s the end goal of my cooking and baking recipes, how to have some decent dessert, or food but also being healthy.

“The secret of success in life is to eat what you like and let the food fight it out inside.” - American author Mark Twain
