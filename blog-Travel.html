Visiting New Places

What are best places to visit

February 03, 2020

I crave adventure. I think travel is a way of getting out of a consistent routine. Travel doesn’t just mean go somewhere far with a precise plan beforehand, you can just pack your bag and go to somewhere spontaneous. traveling is challenging, it’s going Out of your comfort zone and making new memories, new experience, meeting new people, having a local authentic dish of food, seeing picturesque landscapes and capsuling those moments in your mind and soul. Traveling gives you peace of mind to think deeper, it exposes you to different people different ideas different cultures, through all these you can discover your passions , solve unsolved problems and become someone different than a moment before. Here, I want to share places I visit which worth your time and I want to give some information on what will you need in there, what to expect, and what to consider if you want to visit them. I decided to list places that every one suggest to visit, everywhere, from where I live (Boston) to other states and other countries around the world, if you can add to this list it will be really goooood

“The gladdest moment in human life is a departure into unknown lands.” – Sir Richard Burton
“Travel makes one modest. You see what a tiny place you occupy in the world.” -Gustav Flaubert
“Traveling – it leaves you speechless, then turns you into a storyteller.” – Ibn Battuta
