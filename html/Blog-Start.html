LET NEW adventures BEGIN

"If anything is important enough to you, you will find a way to make time for it." Find your passions, and hold tight to your passion and there is the success. When you find those passions in you, consider them as challenges and those challenges makes your life meaningful. I am writing this blog because I believe that more you give attention to your passions, more real they become, and I am a believer in what Monahan Said: "The more people you bring into the fold to share your goals with, the more people you have asking you about your progress and updates, which keeps you on track," . if you want to learn new skills, start doing new hobby, or any other new adventure in your life journey,take action rather than delaying it to unknown and undefined free time in future that you know it will never come as perfect as you expect it to be .you are never going to be as free as you want to sit somewhere and following your dreams. There is a point however, try to be realistic about your passions. most of us have a lot of different passions to follow, but first you need to have a big plan for your future and pick new interests along with your future goal, unless you are going to waste your time learning a lot of different new things that your playful mind wants to go after, and you will end up knowing a little from hundreds of topics.

So here I am, writing about things I am passionate to learn or start learning (new exciting hobbies, activities, skills and anything new as general), so it may be a motivation for me or even one person to start following her/his dreams. please share if any idea or suggestions you have in comment area. Thank you

“Always walk through life as if you have something new to learn, and you will.” Vernon Howard
