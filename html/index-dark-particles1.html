About Me.

Civil & Environmental Engineer

I’m a doctoral student (Ph.D.) in Civil Environmental Engineering department of Northeastern University. My Ph.D. dissertation project focuses on removing contamination using a flow-through electrochemical reactor. We are testing different cathode material, voltage and different flow rates.This subject is extremely fascinating, and I am so excited doing this project.

Before joining Northeastern, I worked in structural company for a couple of years. We were designing different types of structures from parking to mega malls.

while studying MSc, I worked as a research and teaching assistant at K. N. Toosi University of Technology. My master thesis was "Investigating Methods of Petroleum Wastewater Treatment by the Combination of Activated Sludge, Rotating Biological Contractor (RBC) and Sand Filtration with the Objective of Reuse" and it was Funded by National Iranian Oil Co., Ministry of Energy, Tehran, IRAN.

I'm looking forward to hearing anything that helps me improve scientifically from other colleagues, also I am absolutely eager to help others if I have the knowledge. So, please feel free to contact me.
